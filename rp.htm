Religious Places



Church of Jesus Christ

Members and missionaries of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Bishoftu, Ethiopia, welcomed about 50 members of the public to an open house held at the local meetinghouse on Saturday 28 May. Bishoftu has about 300,000 people and is home to the third branch of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the country of Ethiopia. “Finished in 2013, the Bishoftu chapel quickly became the centre of much attention among the citizens of the town,” said Joseph Nelson, a missionary serving in the Ethiopia Addis Ababa mission.

Ifa Fayyina International Church

Ifa Fayyina International Church is a church founded by a man of God, Apostle Getahun Immiru, in the Bishoftu East Showa zone of Oromia State, Ethiopia, after receiving a vision from God. Ifa Fayina International Church was established in July 2018 with the primary goal of preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations in accordance with the commandments of Our Lord Jesus Christ. The church is located at Bishoftu city in Oromia near to capital city of Ethiopia. You can find our church junction near too Getshet.
