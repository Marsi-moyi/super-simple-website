General Info

Bishoftu is center of Culture, Turism and Industry. Bishoftu is a young city of 70 years of age and the term Bishoftu came from "bishaanooftuu", which translates to "watery" (place of water/lakes/springs). There are seven lakes which is popular recreational destinations from the nearby capital city of Ethiopia Finfine or Addis Ababa.
Bishoftu is located in the state of Oromia, Ethiopia, approximately 47 km south east of Finfine or Addis Ababa. Its topography is undulating and characterized by flat land on the north and east parts of the city is locked by several lakes, while the south is dominated by hills.
