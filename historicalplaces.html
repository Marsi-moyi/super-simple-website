      Irrecha Hora Arsadi

Irreechaa is the annual Oromo people Thanksgiving Day that celebrated every year in Birraa near the river bank or water and tree. Irreechaa is celebrated every year in September in Bishoftu Hora Harsadii and other Oromia major cities. One of the distinct thanksgiving celebrations in the Oromo tradition is called “Irreechaa” or “Irreessaa,” which, by itself, means ‘green and fresh grass’ that symbolise fertility and flourishing life due to the bless and guidance of the Creator, Waaqaa.








Chukala Mount

Mount Zuqualla or Chuqqaala is an extinct volcano in the Oromia Region, Situated in Bishoftu, it rises from the plain 30 km south of Bishoftu. With a height of 2,989 metres (9,806 ft), it is known for its crater lake, lake Dembel, an elliptical crater lake with a maximum diameter of about one kilometer, but the trail around the crater is about 6 kilometers long.
