General Info

Bishoftu is center of Culture and Turism. Bishoftu is a young town of 70 years of age. It was founded in 1937 by Italian invaders as a center of colonization and as an experimental region for agricultural activities. There are seven lakes which is popular recreational destinations from the nearby capital city of Ethiopia Finfine or Addis Ababa.
Bishoftu is located in the state of Oromia, Ethiopia, approximately 47 km south east of Finfine or Addis Ababa. Its topography is undulating and characterized by flat land on the north and east parts of the city, locked by several lakes, while the south is dominated by hills.
