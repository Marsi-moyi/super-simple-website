Bishoftu Info Website



The aim of this website is just for giving information about Bishoftu city!



In current times, each and every person wishes to travel Bishoftu. Because Bishoftu is a center of Culture and Turism. On the other hand there are many luxury Resorts and around seven lakes found. It is a great concern on how to plan the whole travel keeping in mind that both money and time are spent wisely. Bishoftu is famous for skydiving, jet skiing, sailboats, water parks, boat rides, museums, swimming, farms, volcanoes, escape room, hammam, skiing, theme parks, amusement parks, natural places, nature, festival, surf, docks, lakes, shooting ranges, canoeing, beaches, music, biking, off-road trails in Ethiopia.

So this website has been created in order to provide accurate information to the visitors regarding all the tourist places, hotels, resorts, religious places in the city and their stay along with their official respective websites linked to it. This will help to get all the detailed and legitimate information regarding it. Hope you enjoy planning your travel through this website.
