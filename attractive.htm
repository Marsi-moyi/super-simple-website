Salayish Lodge and Park

Located on the edge of tranquil Lake Babugaya, Salayish Lodge and Park is the perfect place to relax during your stay in Ethiopia. The lodge consists of six traditionally-built bamboo tukuls in a lush garden filled with avocado, mango, and coffee trees, and one deluxe honeymoon suite with a panoramic view over the lake and surrounding countryside.
